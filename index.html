Xi Luo

I am a graduate student pursuing a Master’s degree in Mathematical and Theoretical Physics at the University of Oxford. I previously completed my Bachelor’s degree in Physics with First-Class Honors at the University of Manchester, where I developed a strong foundation in Quantum Mechanics, Electromagnetism, and Advanced Quantum Field Theory. My research experience includes investigating Higgs self-coupling in the Standard Model and Beyond, contributing to the CMS High Granularity Calorimeter upgrade, and leveraging machine learning techniques such as SPANet for Higgs decay reconstruction. I am proficient in Python, ROOT, and MadGraph, with hands-on experience in experimental setups and electronic system analysis.
